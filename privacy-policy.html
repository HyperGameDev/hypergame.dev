Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13th, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to HyperGame.Dev! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide: When you fill out forms on our website (e.g., joining a queue or contacting us), we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and message.
  • Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we may collect your IP address and browsing behavior using cookies or similar technologies.
  • Third-Party Information: If you interact with embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos), third-party services may collect information about you. We do not control or have access to this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • To manage queues for livestreams and notify you when it's your turn to share your project.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To send you newsletters or updates (only if you explicitly opt-in).

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Queue Data: Data submitted for livestream queues is deleted within 24 hours of the stream's completion.
  • Contact Form Data: Data submitted through contact forms is retained until your inquiry is resolved.
  • Newsletter Data: If you opt-in to our newsletter, your email address will be retained until you unsubscribe.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • YouTube: Embedded YouTube videos may use cookies to track your viewing behavior. We do not control or have access to this data. For more information, please review YouTube's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify: We use Netlify to process form submissions. Netlify may collect your IP address and form data. For more information, please review Netlify's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (e.g., GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving newsletters or updates at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the Contact Form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the Contact Form.
