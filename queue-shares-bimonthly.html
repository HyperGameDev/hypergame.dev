FAQ

What is this?

Sign up for the free newsletter here: https://www.patreon.com/HyperGameDev.
One week ahead of the stream, you'll receive an email with the Unlisteed livestream URL. Then join the queue on this page to have your game played during that next feedback stream!

Can I only share games?

Game work in progress elements like art and music are okay too! Games will be given the most time however.

How will I know if I'm next?

Just ask!

Can I submit multiple times?

Only if you actually have another thing to share, please. There is a two share maximum for these feedback streams.
